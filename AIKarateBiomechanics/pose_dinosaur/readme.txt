Usage Instructions:


Allow Camera Access: The browser will prompt you to allow camera access. Make sure to grant permission.

Interact with the App:

Pose Detection: You should see your webcam feed with pose landmarks and connections drawn over it.
Elbow Angle: Move your right arm to see the elbow angle displayed.
Dinosaur Appearance: Bring your hands together (so that the wrists are close) to trigger the dinosaur explosion.
Troubleshooting Tips:

Good Lighting: Ensure you're in a well-lit environment for better pose detection accuracy.

  Camera Permissions: Verify that your browser has permission to access the webcam.
Console Errors: If something isn't working, check the browser's developer console for any errors or warnings.
